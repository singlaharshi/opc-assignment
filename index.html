02 — Distribution & Channel Strategy

One clear entry point.
Everything else follows.

The app may contain Baskets, Mutual Funds, FDs, Insurance from day one, but that doesn't mean we market all of them together. When everything is the hero, nothing is. Every few weeks, a specific product gets its own spotlight through dedicated content, creator partnerships, onboarding flows, and community activation.

The waitlist isn't passive. The FIRE calculator isn't a form. Every touchpoint is designed to give the user a reason to act before the app even launches.

Pre-Launch Waitlist — 3 Touches Only

1

Day of Signup

WhatsApp from the 1% Club number: "You're in. We're building something that'll show you exactly when you can stop working." No product pitch. No link. A human moment.

2

One Week Before Launch

"Find your FIRE number before anyone else does." Links to a web-based FIRE calculator — no app download required. First product taste before the app is live.

3x more likely to convert on launch day
3

Launch Day

"It's live. You're early. Here's your link." A direct deep link bypassing the app store browse entirely. Zero friction between message and first session.
