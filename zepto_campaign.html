02

The Core Insight

The lottery is the most potent symbol of the mindset 1% Club is fighting against — the idea that wealth arrives in a lucky moment, not through deliberate action. This card makes you experience that belief, then dismantles it in a single sentence. The joke is the lesson.

The Campaign Structure
A lesson in two beats.
The setup makes you believe. The punchline makes the point. You can't get to the second beat without living through the first — which is exactly why it lands.
Beat 01 — The Setup
You've won ₹3 crore.
The card looks exactly like a government lottery. Official header, serial number, gold foil panels, fine print, the full production. All three panels match. The moment of belief is real — even if brief. That fraction of a second matters.
"Congratulations! You won ₹3 crore."
Beat 02 — The Lesson
Wealth isn't won. It's built.
The fine print: "Unfortunately, this is just a scratch card. Turn over for the real thing." The back drops the joke and makes the point. A QR code goes straight to the FIRE calculator. No download, no friction — just the number.
"Wealth isn't won. It's built. Find your FIRE age."
Why the joke is essential, not optional The emotional jolt of expecting ₹3 crore and reading "it's just a scratch card" makes the insight hit harder than any headline copy could. You've just lived the exact mindset the card is critiquing — believing wealth comes in a lucky moment. The experience is the argument. That's something a standard insert cannot do.
Execution
Every detail exists to protect the moment of belief.
The card has to look real. If it reads as a marketing insert before the scratch, the joke dies. Every production and placement decision is in service of that one second.
Card material
Premium matte card stock — not glossy, not cheap. The tactile quality signals legitimacy. It should feel like something official when you first pick it up.
Scratch panels
Real gold foil scratch-off coating. Not printed graphics, not sticker panels. Actual scratch material that reveals the ₹1Cr amounts underneath when scratched. The act of scratching is part of the belief formation.
Front design
Government lottery aesthetic — official seal, unique serial number ("BK-4782910"), series code ("GOLD-A"), "Govt. of India Authorised," prize terms fine print. The closer to real it looks, the better the beat-one reaction.
Placement in bag
Placed on top of items by the Zepto delivery partner before the bag is sealed — not buried at the bottom, not in a pocket. First thing you see when you open the bag. The discovery is the moment.
QR on the reverse
UTM-tagged QR code links to the web-based FIRE calculator. No app store, no sign-up. City-specific UTM parameters so Bengaluru, Mumbai, and Delhi conversion rates are tracked independently.
Run duration
2 weeks around launch. Orders above ₹300 in Bengaluru, Mumbai, Delhi. These three cities have the highest ICP concentration in the Zepto user base.
The Partnership Rationale
Why Zepto. Why this format. Why now.
🎯
Perfect ICP overlap
Zepto's salaried metro demographic in Bengaluru, Mumbai, Delhi is a near-perfect match for the Stuck Saver — age 26–36, ₹10L+, urban, tech-comfortable, ordering groceries at 9pm. No targeting algorithm needed. The channel self-selects.
🏠
Right context and timing
The card arrives at home, not mid-commute. The person is relaxed, not in transit. They open the bag, discover the card, and have the time and mental space to scratch it and actually read the back. High-quality attention.
📸
Inherently shareable
"Bhai Zepto ke andar yeh aaya" will happen before the back is read. People photograph surprising things. That WhatsApp message or Instagram story — which cost nothing — reaches people who never received the card. The earned media is the actual ROI.
⚡
Zero-friction product entry
QR → web FIRE calculator → number. No app store, no account creation, no form. The first experience of 1% Club is the core product value in under 2 minutes. Someone who finds their FIRE age at 9:30pm opening a Zepto bag is a highly primed lead.
The Case For It
Why this belongs in the GTM.
01 — Distribution
The joke is the media buy
The social share moment — "dekho kya Zepto ke andar aaya" — happens before the QR is even scanned. Every organic post extends reach to people who never received the card. The earned media compounds. The insert becomes the campaign.
02 — Memory
Surprising things are remembered
Expecting ₹3 crore and reading "it's just a scratch card" creates a small emotional jolt. Surprising experiences stick. When someone hears "1% Club" a week later, this card is the memory that surfaces. That's brand recall no banner ad achieves.
03 — Persuasion
Show, don't tell
"Wealth isn't won, it's built" is a marketing line. Experiencing the lottery fantasy and having it pulled away is a lesson. You've acted out the belief the card critiques. The insight lands because you've just lived it — not because someone told you.
04 — Brand fit
Pitch-perfect positioning
1% Club is built against financial shortcuts, get-rich-quick thinking, and platforms that serve themselves before the user. A campaign that literally subverts the lottery — the ultimate shortcut fantasy — is direct, honest, and on-brand in every pixel.
The Governing Principle The scratch card is not a marketing insert. It's a complete acquisition funnel in one physical object: distribution (Zepto bag), awareness (lottery aesthetic), earned reach (social share), consideration (beat-one belief), conversion (beat-two + QR), and first product experience (FIRE calculator). All in one card.
